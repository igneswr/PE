How To Play

Each match begins with all players spawning as humans. After a short delay, one randomly selected player becomes the first zombie. Their objective is to infect all humans before they escape. Humans must explore the map quickly, searching buildings, containers, and loot areas to find weapons, ammunition, armor, and useful equipment needed for survival.

As humans fight and survive, they must work together to reach the train before time runs out. The train is the only way to escape and win the round as a human. Once the train arrives, humans must board it and defend themselves until departure. If a human fails to board the train in time, they will be left behind and lose.

If a zombie successfully infects a human, that player joins the zombie team and helps hunt the remaining survivors. Zombies grow stronger as their numbers increase, making survival progressively harder for humans.

Humans earn stars and rewards by surviving rounds, escaping, killing zombies, or completing objectives. These rewards can be used to unlock perks, costumes, and gameplay enhancements that improve survivability or provide strategic advantages.

The game is heavily focused on teamwork, movement skill, map knowledge, and decision making. Humans must balance looting, fighting, and escaping, while zombies must coordinate attacks, ambush survivors, and prevent escape.

Zombie Classes

Catcher (Default) Balanced zombie class with reliable damage and mobility.
Mimic Can disguise as humans to deceive survivors.
Phantom Can turn invisible temporarily to ambush humans.
Bomber Explodes to deal massive damage to humans.

Human Perks

Rebellious (Default) Spawn with +1 armor.
Doubtful Carry 3 guns, third gun deals increased damage.
Lover Gain lifesteal when shooting zombies.
Safety Spawn with 5 armor and 1 heart. Armor and hearts regenerate.
Vanity Triple jump instead of double jump.
Leader Move faster and camouflage while crouching/sliding.
Hopeful Regenerate health and revive zombies into humans. 2 uses per round.
