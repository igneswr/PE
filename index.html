How To Play

Every match begins with all players starting as humans, except for one randomly selected player who becomes the initial zombie. The humans must explore the map, search buildings, and loot weapons, equipment, and supplies to defend themselves.

The main objective for humans is to survive and reach the escape train before time runs out. Once the train arrives, humans must board it to escape and win the round. However, zombies will attempt to infect every remaining human before they can escape.

When a human is killed or infected by a zombie, they respawn as a zombie and join the infected team. This causes the zombie team to grow stronger as the match progresses, increasing pressure on the remaining survivors.

Humans can unlock and wear unique costumes using stars. Stars can be earned by winning matches or simply playing the game. Some costumes provide special perks and bonuses, allowing players to customize their playstyle and improve survival chances.

Zombies can choose between different zombie types, each with unique abilities:

Bomber

Can explode to deal massive area damage to humans.

Catcher

A balanced zombie with high damage and reliable combat ability.

Mimic

Can disguise itself as a human to trick and ambush survivors.

Phantom

Can turn invisible temporarily to sneak up on humans.

Humans win by successfully escaping on the train. Zombies win by infecting every human before escape. Each round is fast-paced, intense, and different every time depending on player actions and strategy.
